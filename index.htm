About

Hailing from Long Island, New York, Anoint the Sick is a progressive hardcore band that favors a style that evokes the guitar histrionics of late 90s metal with brutal breakdowns found in today's hardcore while also balancing doses of darkness and melody.

The group features members of several prominent punk/hardcore bands including Skycamefalling, The Backup Plan, Subterfuge and This Is Hell.
